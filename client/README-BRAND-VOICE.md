## BRAND VOICE

> Project: Suitcase Home

The project **Suitecase** is birth from the necessity to offer workers of the **public services** and the **informal sector** a means to build their house despite their low state of income per month. To ensure this possibilty to not be just hypothetical, our plan is to plit allthe civil engineering work into several quotations plan to help the customers to arrange specific budget to each phase to realize.

#### WHY WE DECIDE TO DO THIS IN FIRST PLACE?

Unhappily in the new **economical tendency** of the society where we are moving on. Worth are push away when it get too think in sympathy about working to help people fulfill one of their dream. Our need is to get back to connect people with a phase of their life where they could experience true happiness. To help them construct their houses, give them places where every moment that they pass under these shelves, they remember that life is not just about chasing materials, but also staging endeavor to connect with others humans being.
